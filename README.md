# ✨ Diwali Greeting 2025

**Diwali Greeting 2025** is an interactive festive web experience created to celebrate the joy, warmth, and beauty of Diwali through animation, sound, lights, and heartfelt wishes.

Instead of being a simple greeting page, this project turns the celebration into a small journey. Users enter the celebration, light diyas, launch crackers and rockets, read warm blessings, and replay or share the experience.

---

## 🎆 Project Highlights

This project is designed as a **multi-scene festive celebration website** with immersive visual and interactive elements.

### What users can do
- Enter a Diwali celebration experience
- Light diyas one by one
- Unlock blessing messages while lighting lamps
- Watch fireworks, rockets, chakris, and flowerpot effects
- Read a beautiful Diwali wishes message
- Replay the celebration
- Share the experience with others

---

## 🌟 Features

### 🪔 Diya Lighting Scene
Users can click on each diya to light it.  
Every diya reveals a blessing line, and once all diyas are lit, a special festive message appears.

### 🎇 Fireworks Celebration
The project includes a sky celebration scene with:
- Rocket launches
- Chakri spinning effect
- Flowerpot firework effect
- Click-based manual fireworks

### 🎵 Audio Support
The experience supports:
- Background music
- Diya lighting sound effects
- Firework burst sounds
- Click interaction sounds

It also includes fallback logic for audio behavior.

### 💌 Blessings & Wishes
A dedicated message scene displays warm and touching Diwali wishes that make the project feel personal and heartfelt.

### 🔁 Replay & Share
Users can replay the full celebration or share the page link with loved ones.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

The website is divided into multiple scenes:

1. **Entry Scene** – welcome screen to begin the celebration  
2. **Diyas Scene** – users light each diya interactively  
3. **Crackers Scene** – rockets and festive firework animations  
4. **Blessings Scene** – heartfelt Diwali message  
5. **Closing Scene** – replay or share the celebration  

The logic is handled using JavaScript scene transitions, interactive event handling, and DOM-based festive particle effects.
